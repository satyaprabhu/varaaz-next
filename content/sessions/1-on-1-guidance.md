## Session Overview

Get personalized feedback and instruction in a one-on-one session with our experienced instructors. Whether you're working through a course or pursuing independent study, we're here to help you overcome challenges and accelerate your progress.

## What's Included

- Personalized assessment of your current level
- Custom guidance based on your goals
- Real-time feedback on your work
- Tips and techniques tailored to your needs
- Encouragement and support from an experienced artist

## Perfect For

- Beginners looking for foundational guidance
- Intermediate painters wanting to refine techniques
- Advanced artists seeking specialized feedback
- Anyone struggling with specific techniques or concepts
- Artists preparing for exhibitions or commissions

## Session Format

Sessions are conducted via video call. You'll share your work (photos of paintings or sketches) and discuss your artistic goals. Our instructors will provide practical advice and demonstrate techniques as needed.

## How to Book

1. Choose your preferred instructor (if available)
2. Select your desired session date and time
3. Make payment securely via Razorpay
4. Receive calendar invite and meeting link
5. Prepare your work and questions
6. Enjoy your personalized session

## Rescheduling Policy

Sessions can be rescheduled up to 48 hours in advance. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.
